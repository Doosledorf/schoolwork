Welcome to Northrend

Greetings, I present to you Northrend AKA the "Crown of Azeroth."

As it exists in Warcraft lore, I have chosen it as one of my favorite place, mainly because its story surrounds one guy, the Lich King AKA Arthas Menethil. As you will find out, he is one of those tragic-hero types and I'm a sucker for the tragic-hero archetype. Besides that, Northrend just oozes flavor. Every zone is immersive, but they all have this air of majesty about them. There is so much deeply rooted history. Whether its from Sargeras' battle in The Storm Peaks or Galakrond's skeleton in Dragonblight, you can't help but feel part of it. And when you storm Icecrown Citadel...its just a rush.

Lets get on with the show.
