The Nexus | The Oculus

Minimum Level: 68 and 75 respectively

Loacation: Borean Tundra

Final Boss: Keristrasza (Nexus) and Ley-Guardian Eregos (Oracle)

As the Guardian of Magic, Malygos seeks to put a cap on the magic use throughout Azeroth. Using the Arcanomicon, he taps into the ley-lines of the world and begins to siphon it out into the Twisting Nether via the use of the Nexus. This leads to major rift-tearing and its up to the group to stop him.

Loacation: Icecrown

Final Boss: The Devourer of Souls, Scourgelord Tyrannus, The Lich King

Players storm Icecrown Citadel with intentions of sabotaging a "Soul-Grinder" production facility (Forge of Souls), rescue prisoners of the Scourge (Pit of Saron) and seek out the Lich King himself(Halls of Reflection).
